You can purchase your ticket online by Booking a trip, or pay at the bus, with a debt or credit card.

Discounted Tickets

BC Residents and passenger 65 years or older, with a valid form of governamental issued ID, with photo and birthdate, are eleigible for a 10% discount on any ticket, to any of our intinerary.

If you are registered as a "loyal customer" you are elegible for a free ticket too

Changes and Cancelations

You can cancel or make changes to your reservantion up to 6 hours before the depature time of your online purchased ticket.

You can not cancel or make changes to tickets purchased at the bus.

Late & no Show Policy

If you are late and miss the bus, we may be able to accommodate you on the next bus on the same day to the same destination, if space is available.

You must call us to let us know you will not make it on time, so we can hold a seat on the next bus if still available at the time you contact us.

As a courtesy, our drivers will always wait for 5 extra minutes after departure time, in case any of our passengers with a reservation is late. But never any longer than 5 minutes, as we need to maintain our schedules.
