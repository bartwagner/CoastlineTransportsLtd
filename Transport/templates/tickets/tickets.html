Buying a ticket

You can purchase your ticket online by Booking a trip, or pay at the bus, with a debt or credit card.

Online booking can be made up to 1 hour before the chosen departure time.

Unfortunately for safety reasons, our drivers are not allowed to accept cash payments.

Discounted Tickets

BC Residents and passenger 65 years or older, with a valid form of governamental issued ID, with photo and birthdate, are eleigible for a 10% discount on any ticket, to any of our destinations.

If you are registered as a "loyal customer" you are elegible for a free ticket too

Changes and Cancelations

You can cancel or make changes to your reservantion up to 6 hours before the depature time of your ticket purchased online.

If you are a registered for loyalty program, you can change or cancel up to 2 hours before departure time.

You can not cancel or make changes to tickets purchased at the bus.

If you are late

If you are late and miss the bus, we may be able to accommodate you on the next bus on the same day to the same destination, if space is available.

You must call us as soon as possible to let us know you will be late, so we can hold an available seat on the next bus for you.

As a courtesy, our drivers will wait for 5 extra minutes after departure time, if you call and let us know you are coming drivers will not waite any longer than 5 minutes, as we need to maintain our schedules.
