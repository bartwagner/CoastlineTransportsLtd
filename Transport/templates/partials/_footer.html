About Us

Discovery Bus was created to accommodate the public demand for reliable, safe and confortable transportation service, to connect passengers to all corners of the Island.

We offer innercity bus service from Victoria to Port Hardy to Tofino, with diferent routes connecting the main cities, towns and vilaggers along the Island.

Discovery Bus also offer shipping services trought all of our routes. Please contact us for more information on this service.

And we rent out vans for charter services as wedings, day trips, and more.
